An Avengers Appreciation Site

Welcome to the Avengers appreciation page! Here we'll go through of our favorite Episodes together! Re-caps, fun facts and reviews will follow!

The Avengers is a British television show that ran from the 1950's through the 1960's. It follows the escapades of secret agents Steed and Mrs. Peel as they thwart baddies, stop nefarious plans, and fight all kinds of strange science fiction machines!

When I was a kid, my grandma and I would sit and watch these episodes on VHS! It was one of our favorite things to do together and I love the show to this day! I just introduced my partner to the show and now we are enjoying watching together so I decided to make this page as a place to log our experience with the show!
