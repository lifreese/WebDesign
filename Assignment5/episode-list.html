-Season Four: Part 01-

Episodes: 01-13

Episode 01: The Town of no Return

Steed and Mrs. Peel, first introduced in this very episode, are assigned to the quaint town Little Baisley by the Sea, after all the prior agents assigned to investigate the area have disappeared. The town itself seems to be almost abandoned with the remaining citizens acting strangley to the duo. What could this little town be hiding?

Episode 02: The Gravediggers

Steed and Peel investigate a series of radio jammers planted strategically around a missile base in the surrounding town's cemetaries. A suspicious hospital for railway men, odd burials, and an excenteric millionare train officianado with his very own miniature train engine ride are just a few of the clues exumed during this mystery!

Episode 03: The Cybernauts

A tall, seemingly invincible being is tageting and killing tech company leaders, surving being shot and breaking through barriers with ease, and escaping every time. Will Steed and Mrs. Peel stop the violent assasin in time?

Episode 04: Death at Bargain Prices

A department store is hiding a dark secret and Steed and Mrs. Peel are on the case! Missing physisists, coded receipts, and nasty store management are all a part of this mystery. This episode is one of my favorites!

Episode 05: Castle De'ath

It's off to Scotland for McSteed and Peel as they investigate a castle that keeps having mysterious deaths surrounding it. Ghosts, dungeons, sword fights and kilts await the Avengers as they go undercover as a writer and a castle tourism expert. Is Black Jamie's ghost behind it all? Or is there something more nefarious hidden within the walls of Castle De'ath?

Episode 06: The Masterminds

A heist gone wrong exposes that Brittain's greatest intellectuals are starting to commit crimes, seemingly in a trance like state. All of this leads back to Ransack, an 'intellectual club for eggheads' which Mrs. Peel, a natural genius, and Steed (a genius via cheating), must infiltrate and investigate.

Episode 07: The Murder Market

Singles meeting for arranged dates only to be murdered by one another? The 'Happiness' Marraige Beuro lays at the center of this twisted romance web. Steed and Peel must both sign up right away to meet their deadly dates and find out the master scheme of the Marriage Beuro, before more unwitting Singles hearts stop beating.

Episode 08: A Surfeit of H2O

People are drowning in the middle of empty fields in a matter of minutes, seemingly out of nowhere. Steed and Peel must investigate the rural area whose inhabitants include a modern day Noah building his ark for the end times, a suspicious wine factory hiding secrets, and an innocent farmer whose brother was one of the first victims to fall prey to the mysterious drownings.

Episode 09: The Hour that Never was

Steed and Peel attend a party for the closing of the airbase Steed served in during WWII, only to find no one is there, but all the signs of a party remain. What happened to the people? Has time seemingly stopped? Will Steed and Peel survive this party?

Episode 10: Dial a Deadly Number

Top stock market players are dropping dead out of nowhere with the only thing connecting them being a high tech new pocket pager. Snobby wine officianatos, a serial killer clock maker, and lots of corruption playout through this tense episode.

Episode 11: Man-Eater of Surrey Green

A strange arrival from outer space? Strange acting horticulturists? Villagers disappearing one by one, seemingly in a trance? The Avengers are on the case! What evil could be growing in Surrey Green?

Episode 12: Two's a Crowd

Enemy agent SEV is in town and it's up to the duo to sniff out what nefarious plans he's hoping to execute. Model planes, a bumbling embassy advisor, and Steeds Doppleganger all feature in this comedic episode.

Episode 13: Too Many Christmas Trees

Steed is having strange dreams of a murderous Father Christmas stalking him through a forest of Christmas trees, and now it seems the dreams are coming true. In one of the creepiest Avengers episodes of all time, Steed and Emma are off to a famous publishers manor in the country for their holiday festivities! But something more sinister is lying just under the surface, threatening to drive Steed into insanity.
